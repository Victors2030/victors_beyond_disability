About Us

Victors Beyond Disability Kenya is a community-based organization dedicated to advocating for the rights, empowerment, and inclusion of persons with disabilities (PWDs). We believe that disability is not inability and strive to create an inclusive society where individuals with disabilities can thrive and achieve their full potential.

Our Mission

To promote the dignity, independence, and social integration of persons with disabilities through advocacy, education, and economic empowerment programs.

Our Vision

A society where persons with disabilities are fully included, respected, and empowered to live fulfilling lives.

Our Core Objectives

  • Advocacy & Awareness – Educating communities about disability rights and breaking stigma.
  • Education & Skills Development – Providing learning opportunities and vocational training.
  • Economic Empowerment – Supporting income-generating activities for PWDs.
  • Healthcare & Assistive Devices – Ensuring access to medical services and mobility aids.
  • Inclusive Events – Organizing activities like the Autism Rally to raise awareness and foster inclusion.
